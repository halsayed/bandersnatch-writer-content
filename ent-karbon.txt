Nutanix Karbon is a certified Kubernetes distribution that simplifies moving Kubernetes into production by automating the provisioning, operations, upgrades and scaling of Kubernetes in a HA configuration.Karbon is more than Kubernetes; it's a complete cloud-native solution that includes necessary components including monitoring, logging, storage, and more.@@pic/karbon-1.png(6.3)@@1.Karbon enables enterprises to provide a private-cloud Kubernetes solution with the simplicity and performance of public clouds.2.Karbon is part of a complete Cloud Native solution from Nutanix including storage (Volumes/Buckets/Files), database automation (Era), and enhanced monitoring (Epoch). 3.Easy installation of Production-HA Kubernetes clusters in just a few minutes.4.Simplified Kubernetes platform operations day 0 and day 1+ (including upgrading, scaling up/down, etc.)5.Easy management of Kubernetes platform – including Kubernetes, networking, monitoring, scaling up/down, and logging stack.6.Leveraging Karbon, developers can enjoy the native Kubernetes experience that is delivered fast while all complexities of infrastructure are abstracted with no additional costs. Karbon is included in all AOS software editions.
