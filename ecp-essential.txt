Nutanix Essentials includes everything in Nutanix Core, plus:  

- Nutanix Flow – Software-defined networking with native micro-segmentation to protect individual applications from internal and external security threats 

- Nutanix Files – Integrated scale-out storage for unstructured file data 

- Nutanix Calm – Built-in application management and orchestration, with self-service capabilities 

- Prism Pro – Advanced infrastructure management, with centralized upgrades, capacity planning and scheduled reporting capabilities
